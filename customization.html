CUSTOMIZATION

We offer custom printing on nearly all of the products we offer!

This means that you can have custom equipment that's 100% unique to you. If you're ordering for your gym, we can print your gym's name and/or logo on nearly any equipment.

Pricing on custom orders will vary based on the type and extent of customization, and time-to-ship will increase due to custom production, but there's really nothing like opening up a shipment of equipment that's 100% your own.

For a no-obligation estimate on custom equipment, contact us with a message including:

  • Product to be customized
  • An estimate of how many pieces
  • Desired custom elements (gym name, logo, color, etc.)
